# Automation Accelerator Pack — Quick Start Guide

## What Is the Automation Accelerator Pack?

The Automation Accelerator Pack is a complete toolkit for launching, selling, and delivering automation services to clients. It includes workflow templates, client proposal frameworks, pricing calculators, onboarding scripts, and email sequences — everything you need to start closing automation deals in your first week.

## Who Is This For?

This pack is designed for:

- **Freelancers** wanting to add automation services to their offerings
- **Agency owners** looking to productize and scale automation delivery
- **Consultants** who want a repeatable system for client projects
- **Solo operators** building a service business around automation
- **Anyone** tired of reinventing the wheel for every client engagement

## Implement in 10 Minutes

1. **Download the pack** — All files are ready to use immediately
2. **Review the Quick Start Guide** (this document)
3. **Open Workflow Templates** — Pick one that fits your first client
4. **Customize the Proposal Template** — Add your branding and pricing
5. **Send your first outreach email** — Templates are pre-written
6. **Book a call** — Use the onboarding scripts to qualify leads
7. **Deliver the automation** — Follow the workflow template step-by-step

## Step-by-Step Workflow Overview

### Phase 1: Lead Generation (Day 1)
- Use email templates to reach out to 10 potential clients
- Focus on businesses with manual, repetitive processes
- Track responses in your CRM or Notion dashboard

### Phase 2: Discovery Call (Day 2-3)
- Use the First Client Call script
- Identify 3 automation opportunities
- Qualify using the Automation Qualification checklist

### Phase 3: Proposal (Day 3-4)
- Use the Client Proposal Template
- Calculate ROI using the Pricing Calculator
- Present a clear scope and timeline

### Phase 4: Delivery (Week 1-2)
- Select a workflow template (Zapier/Make ready)
- Build the automation in 2-4 hours
- Document and hand off to the client

### Phase 5: Follow-Up (Week 3+)
- Send testimonial request email
- Propose upsell opportunities
- Build recurring revenue
